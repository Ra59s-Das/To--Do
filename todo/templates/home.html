FAQ

Common questions.

Is Doable really free?
Yes — the free tier includes unlimited tasks, priority levels, labels, subtasks, search, and filters. No credit card required. Premium features like AI suggestions, analytics, and recurring tasks are part of the Pro plan.
How does the AI suggestion work?
As you type a task title, Claude AI analyses your words and suggests the most appropriate priority level, label category, and due date. You can apply the suggestion in one click or ignore it — you're always in control.
What happens when a recurring task is completed?
When you check off a recurring task, Doable automatically creates the next occurrence with the correct due date (tomorrow, next week, or next month depending on your setting). You never need to recreate it manually.
Can I export my data?
Yes. Pro users can export all tasks to CSV at any time from the analytics page. Your data is always yours and you can take it anywhere.
How does the streak tracker work?
Every day you complete at least one task, your streak count increases. Miss a day and the streak resets. Your longest streak is saved forever as your personal best. It's a simple but powerful motivator to stay consistent.
